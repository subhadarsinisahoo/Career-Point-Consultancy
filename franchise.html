    • Concept :
    Careerpointconsultancy.in offers a complete business format franchise in the educational consultancy services. The unique franchise concept provides individuals with complete know how to build and operate a successful global educational consultancy and IELTS and English Academy.
    Careerpointconsultancy offers its franchisees :

  • Material Support.

    • support :
    Franchise orientation program (FOP) – A 3 day orientation program will be held for training the franchisee and their key personnel. The FOP aims at helping the franchisee understand the educational consultancy business and transferring the knowledge ware gathered by the Franchiser.
    Counselors Training (CT) – Specially developed training module for counselors on admission prospecting. Counselors Training is imparted on a regular basis. New appointees are also trained by us at no extra cost.
    Brochures and Promotional Items – Franchisee will be provided with Brochures and Promotional Items to promote our programmes.
    On going support – The benefits of on going research and development will be transferred to the franchisee to keep abreast of the curriculum development.
    Marketing support – The franchisor will provide requisite marketing support to promote the business through regional/national platforms. Sales & Marketing tools (brochures, leaflets, posters, audio visual aids) as and when developed will be sent as per policy.
