MCA

MCA course is a three year Post-Graduate program in Computer Applications,Approved by Technical All India Council for Education (AICTE).

The MCA course consists of core subjects like Operating systems, Computer Networks, Unix System Programming, Databases, Mathematics, Professional Communication and Ethics.

The Master of Computer Application curriculum spreads over 6 semesters which includes courses for 5 semesters aimed at transmitting the broad spectrum of knowledge as indicated below and 1 semester industrial training with internships in reputed organizations .
